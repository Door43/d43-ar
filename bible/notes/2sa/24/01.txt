===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **the anger of Yahweh was ignited against Israel** - The word "ignite" means start a fire. AT: "the anger of Yahweh started to burn like a fire"  (See: [[:ar:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he moved David against them** - "he caused David to oppose them"
  * **Dan to Beersheba **- This phrase uses two place names Dan and Beersheba to represent the entire country. (See: [[:ar:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
