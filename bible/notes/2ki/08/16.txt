===== Translation Notes: =====

Jehoram becomes king of Judah.

  * **In the fifth year of Joram, the son of Ahab, king of Israel**  - "During the fifth year of the reign of king Joram, son of Ahab, over Israel"
  * **Jehoram began to reign**  - Jehoram, son of Jehoshaphat, became the king of Judah
