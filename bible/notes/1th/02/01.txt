===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you yourselves**  - The word "you" refers to the Thessalonian believers. (See: [[:ar:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **our coming**  - The word "our" refers to Paul, Silvanus and Timothy but not the believers. (See: [[:ar:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **was not useless**  - "was very worthwhile" (See: [[:ar:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes|Litotes]])
  * **suffered and were shamefully treated**  - Paul was beaten and put into jail at Philippi. AT: " were mistreated and insulted."
  * **among much conflict**  - "while meeting with great opposition"
