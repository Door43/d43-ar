===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaiah continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **We growl like bears and moan like doves**  - These refer to the sounds the people made because they were distressed and mourning. (See: [[:ar:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
