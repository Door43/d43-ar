===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **is upon me** - Here "me" refers to Yahweh's servant.
  * **to proclaim liberty to the captives, and the opening of the prison to those who are bound** - The may mean that the servant will free the people who are in exile. It may also mean the servant will remove their sins.
