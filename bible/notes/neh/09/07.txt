===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **brought him out of Ur of the Chaldees - ** The implied information is that God brought Abram to the promised land, later named Israel. (See: Explicit and Implicit)
  * **gave him the name Abraham -**  As part of his covenant with Abram, God gave him a new identity. Here the meaning of the names Abram and Abraham is important. AT: "changed his name to Abraham". (See: Translate Name)
  * **You found his heart was faithful before you **- Here "his heart" represents Abraham as a person. "Before you" is another way of saying "in your sight" or "in your presence". AT: "You concluded that he was faithful to you," or "You saw that he was faithful". (See: Idiom)
  * **the land of the Canaanites, the Hittites, the Amorites, the Perizzites, the Jebusites, and the Girgashites**  - AT: "the land of the Canaanite, the Hittite, the Amorite, the Perizzite, the Jebusite, and the Girgashite".
  * **You have kept your promise **- AT: "You have made your words stand".
