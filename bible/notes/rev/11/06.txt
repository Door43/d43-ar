===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to strike the earth with every kind of plague **- This phrase refers to causing all kinds of trouble to happen on the earth. (See: [[:ar:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the deep and endless**  - The word "endless" means without a bottom.  Both of these words refer to the pit being very deep. (See: [[:ar:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
